Coconut Cream Pie

"Coconut Cream Pie" by kimberlykv is licensed under CC BY 2.0.

This recipe is a favorite of mine because its something my grandma always makes for me. It"s sentimental and just a really great treat! As well as its super easy to make, and is perfect for any holiday or occasion.

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup shredded sweetened coconut
  • Whipped cream
  • Toasted coconut flakes
      1. For the filling combine coconut milk, heavy cream, sugar, cornstarch, and egg yolks in a saucepan. Cook until thickened, then stir in shredded coconut and vanilla extract.
      2. For assembly pour the filling into a pre-baked pie crust, chill until set, and top with whipped ceam before serving.
